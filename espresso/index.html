the perfect shot of espresso

contrary to popular belief, espresso is not a type of bean or roast, it is simply the method witch is used to brew it. espresso is made forcing extremly hot water under high amounts of pressure trhough very finely ground coffee beans. with a rich dark heart, caramel warm body, and smooth crema, you will have the perfect shot of espresso.

espresso is the base for a variety of drinks now ordered in cafes around the world. the most common being a latte or cappuccino. it is a common misconception that all of these titles are interchangable and can be combined to create any custom drinks someone may want. an intricate hand crafted process is implemented to create each one of these beverages. in the diagram below you may see what i mean.
